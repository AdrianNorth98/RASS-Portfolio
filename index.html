Short-term projects (one month or less, typically under R3,000): A 40/60 split. You pay a 40% deposit to secure your delivery slot and get work started. The remaining 60% is paid immediately before your final, polished files are released.

Long-term projects (more than one month, or over R3,000): A flexible milestone-based payment system. A small initial deposit of up to 20% kicks off the planning phase, followed by smaller payments tied to specific deliverables such as the completion of a chapter. Work moves forward step by step as each milestone is cleared.

Our standard turnaround averages 4 to 7 days for short-term projects. Long-term projects are scheduled based on the milestones agreed upon during onboarding.

If you need delivery within 48 to 72 hours, we can prioritise your work with a rush fee of 20% to 100% depending on the workload involved. We will always be transparent about whether a tight deadline is achievable before any payment is made.
